AI isn't coming for South African businesses — it's already here. But most companies aren't sure where to start, or they're worried it's all hype. It's not. Here are three real ways we're seeing AI transform how local businesses operate:

### 1. Automated Customer Enquiries

AI-powered chatbots can handle 60–80% of routine customer questions — booking confirmations, pricing queries, operating hours — without a single staff member lifting a finger. Your team focuses on the conversations that actually need a human touch.

### 2. Smarter Content and SEO

Google's AI Overviews and tools like ChatGPT search are changing how people find businesses online. If your content isn't structured for AI to understand and cite, you're becoming invisible. We call this Generative Engine Optimisation (GEO), and it's the next evolution of SEO.

### 3. Workflow Automation That Saves Real Hours

From auto-generating invoices to routing leads into your CRM, AI-driven workflows eliminate the repetitive admin tasks that drain your team's energy.
