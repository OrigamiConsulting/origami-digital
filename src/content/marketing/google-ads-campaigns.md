## Conversion Tracking Setup

1. **Primary conversion:** Form submission on /get-started/* pages
2. **Secondary conversion:** Contact page form submission
3. **Micro-conversions:** Phone click, email click, blog engagement

### GA4 Events to Track
- `form_submit` — with service type parameter
- `phone_click` — click on phone number
- `cta_click` — click on main CTA buttons
- `scroll_depth` — 25%, 50%, 75%, 100%
