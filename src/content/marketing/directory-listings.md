## Long Description (250 words)

Origami Digital is an AI-augmented digital solutions provider based in Bedfordview, Johannesburg. Founded by Tinashe Munyaka, a senior UI/UX designer and full-stack developer with over a decade of experience, we help South African businesses transform their digital presence.

Our services are organised under three pillars:

**BUILD** — We design and develop high-performance websites and applications using modern technologies like Next.js, React, and TypeScript. Every project is mobile-first, SEO-optimised, and built to convert visitors into customers.

**GROW** — We help businesses rank higher on Google through SEO and Generative Engine Optimisation (GEO), manage Google Ads campaigns, and develop content strategies that drive qualified traffic.

**AUTOMATE** — We leverage AI to streamline business operations, from workflow automation and chatbot development to intelligent document processing and custom internal tools.

What sets us apart is our lean, AI-augmented approach. By working directly with a senior practitioner — without layers of account managers or junior staff — our clients get enterprise-quality digital solutions at a fraction of traditional agency costs. We use the same tools and technologies deployed at companies like ServiceNow and Shopify.

Our portfolio includes projects across web applications, SaaS platforms, EV charging infrastructure dashboards, and B2B consulting sites. We primarily serve established South African SMEs and mid-market companies in professional services, property development, private healthcare, hospitality, and manufacturing.
