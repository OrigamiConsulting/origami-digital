## Step 1 — Open the profile (1 min)

1. Open Chrome → go to https://business.google.com
2. The Origami Digital profile should appear in the dashboard. Click into it.
3. If prompted to verify again, check your preferred verification method (phone call is usually fastest for SA).

## Step 5 — Seed the Q&A section (3 min)

GBP Q&A is an under-used GEO signal — AI search engines do read these.

1. Open the GBP as a customer (use an Incognito window on a different Google account, or ask a team member). The owner account cannot post questions to its own profile.
2. Post these three questions from the non-owner account:

**Q1:** `How much does a website cost?`

**Q2:** `Do you work with businesses outside Johannesburg?`

**Q3:** `What is Generative Engine Optimisation (GEO)?`

3. Switch back to the owner account → you'll see notifications for the new questions → answer each one with the copy below.

**A1 (for "How much does a website cost?"):**
```
Website projects for South African businesses typically fall between R30,000 and R150,000 depending on scope. A small business brochure site starts around R30,000-R50,000, a custom-designed marketing site is R60,000-R120,000, and complex platforms (e-commerce, custom CMS, integrations) range from R120,000 upwards. We publish a detailed pricing guide on our website with full breakdowns: https://origami-digital.co.za/journal/website-cost-south-africa
```

**A2 (for "Do you work with businesses outside Johannesburg?"):**
```
Yes. While we're based in Bedfordview, Johannesburg, we work with businesses across South Africa and increasingly across the SADC region. Modern development workflows make location largely irrelevant — most client communication happens via video call and shared tools.
```

**A3 (for "What is Generative Engine Optimisation (GEO)?"):**
```
GEO is the practice of optimising your website so AI search tools like ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, and Perplexity can understand and recommend your business in their generated answers. Traditional SEO gets you ranked in a list of links; GEO gets you CITED in the AI-generated answer. We build GEO into every website. Learn more: https://origami-digital.co.za/journal/generative-engine-optimisation-south-africa
```
