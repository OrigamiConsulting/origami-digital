# Partnership Outreach — Message Drafts

**👁️ REVIEW-ONLY** — Tinashe reviews these, tweaks where needed, then sends via LinkedIn DM or email.

**Default channel:** LinkedIn (DM the founder/MD — find them via company page → "People" tab)
**Fallback:** Email to info@ or direct to founder email if public

**Tone rules followed:**
- Lead with a specific, non-flattering observation (not "love your work!")
- Make the partnership angle explicit and specific to their business
- Mention the referral fee once, not twice
- Close with a low-friction ask ("happy to grab a coffee / hop on a call / keep in touch")

## Response handling

**If they reply positively:** Book a 30-minute intro call (Calendly link: `origami-digital.co.za/contact` for now, or your direct Calendly). Come with 2-3 example projects to show what your delivery looks like.

**If they reply with questions:** Answer clearly, don't over-pitch. The goal is a first meeting, not closing a deal in DM.

**If they say "send me more info":** Share the `/partners` page link and offer to answer specific questions.

**If they ignore:** One follow-up after 7 days with a specific value-add (a blog post, a specific observation). After that, leave them alone.
