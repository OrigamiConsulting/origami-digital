## Notes for Tinashe

- **Hashtags:** LinkedIn favours 3-5 relevant hashtags, not 10+. Don't stuff.
- **Links:** LinkedIn de-prioritises posts with external links in the main body. Best pattern: post the insight, put the link in the first comment ("Link to the full piece 👇"). Switch to this pattern if engagement drops.
- **Timing:** For JHB B2B, best times are Tuesday–Thursday 08:00–10:00 SAST, or Monday 11:00. Avoid Friday afternoon and weekends.
- **Engagement:** After publishing, respond to EVERY comment within 2 hours for the first 6 hours. LinkedIn's algorithm heavily weights early engagement velocity.
