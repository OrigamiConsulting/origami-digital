## Market observations (for Tinashe's positioning)

A few things stood out while researching:

1. **The SA professional-services market is dominated by two platforms: WordPress (law firms, accountants, physios — often on Elementor or YOOtheme, rarely tuned) and Wix (healthcare specialists, small clinics — capped at whatever schema Wix decides to emit).** Custom Next.js builds are almost non-existent outside the top 1% of firms. This is Origami Digital's core positioning wedge — Tinashe can honestly say "I'm the only developer most of your competitors will ever have seen who's building on a modern stack."

2. **Property developers in Gauteng are near-universally on Prop Data's white-label template.** Visually identical sites across dozens of agencies. A custom front-end over a Prop Data IDX feed is a strong, repeatable pitch.

3. **Nobody — literally nobody in this sample — has FAQPage schema.** FAQ sections exist on maybe 20% of sites, structured FAQ schema on 0%. This is the easiest "GEO audit" finding Tinashe can flag on every single outreach, and it's entirely true.
