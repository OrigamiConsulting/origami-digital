# Runbook — LinkedIn Company Page Setup

**Time required:** 25 minutes
**Auth:** You are already signed in to Google on Chrome with `origamiconsultinggroup@gmail.com` (per your instructions). You'll sign into LinkedIn using that Gmail identity.

**Goal:** Create the Origami Digital LinkedIn Company Page, upload logo, publish the first launch post, and schedule the remaining 3 launch posts.

---

## Step 1 — Sign into LinkedIn (2 min)

1. Open Chrome → go to https://www.linkedin.com/
2. If you are already logged into LinkedIn on your personal profile, skip to Step 2. If not:
   - Click **Sign in**
   - Click **Sign in with Google** (bottom of the sign-in form)
   - Pick `origamiconsultinggroup@gmail.com` from the Google account chooser
   - LinkedIn will ask for basic profile info if this is your first time signing in with this Gmail — fill in the minimum required for a personal account (this is what you'll use to manage the company page)

---

## Step 2 — Create the Company Page (10 min)

1. From the LinkedIn homepage, click the **Work** icon (top right, 9-dot grid) → **Create a Company Page+**
2. Select page type: **Company**
3. Fill in the page details EXACTLY as below — copy-paste these values. Consistency with the website matters for GEO/entity authority.

### Page identity
| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Name | `Origami Digital` |
| LinkedIn public URL | `origami-digital` (check availability; if taken, try `origami-digital-za`) |
| Website URL | `https://origami-digital.co.za` |
| Industry | `IT Services and IT Consulting` |
| Organisation size | `1-10 employees` |
| Organisation type | `Privately held` |

### Profile details (next screen)
| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Tagline | `Build. Grow. Automate.` |
| Logo | Upload `/public/images/logo/origami-horse.png` from this repo (or whichever official logo file you use). Square crop, transparent background ideal. |

### Verify
- Tick the box confirming you are an authorised representative
- Click **Create page**

## Step 4 — Publish launch post #1 (3 min)

1. From the company page, click **Start a post**
2. Paste the launch post from `marketing/linkedin-posts.md` → Post 1
3. **Do NOT include the link in the main post body** — LinkedIn's algorithm de-prioritises external links. Instead:
   - Publish the post WITHOUT the `origami-digital.co.za/contact` link
   - Immediately post a first comment from the Company Page: `Link: origami-digital.co.za/contact`
4. After publishing, share it from Tinashe's personal profile: "Proud to share this" + 2 lines of personal context
5. Respond to EVERY comment within the first 2 hours — this drives algorithmic reach

---

## Step 5 — Schedule posts #2, #3, #4 (2 min)

LinkedIn has basic native scheduling:

1. Click **Start a post** from the company page
2. Paste Post 2 content (from `marketing/linkedin-posts.md`)
3. Click the **clock icon** at the bottom of the composer → **Schedule**
4. Pick a Tuesday morning ~8:30 SAST for best engagement
5. Repeat for Post 3 (2 weeks later) and Post 4 (3 weeks later)

If native scheduling isn't available on your LinkedIn tier, add these to your calendar as reminders instead.

## Troubleshooting

- **"LinkedIn says I need to verify I'm an authorised representative":** Use the official-email verification (hello@origami-digital.co.za) if prompted. You own the domain.
- **Logo doesn't render correctly:** Try a different file format (PNG with transparent background works best). The horse graphic alone is fine as the logo.
- **"Scheduling isn't available":** Post manually and add calendar reminders. Free LinkedIn tier sometimes hides scheduling.
