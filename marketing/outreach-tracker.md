## Pre-send checklist (do per target, takes ~5 min each)

1. **Verify the site issues you're going to mention are still present.** Open the URL, view source, check for schema / FAQ / whatever the draft flags. These change fast.
2. **Run PageSpeed Insights** on `pagespeed.web.dev` — record the mobile LCP number. If the draft has `[LCP]` placeholder, plug it in. If the site has actually fixed the issue, remove that point from the message.
3. **Find the decision-maker on LinkedIn** using the lead in `outreach-targets-week-2.md`. Confirm the person is still at the firm. If they moved on, skip and backfill with another target.
4. **Personalise the first line.** The hook in the draft is baseline — if you spot something more recent (a post from this week, an office move, an award), lead with that instead.
5. **Remove anything that's no longer true.** If their site was rebuilt yesterday, the Week 2 draft won't reflect that — skip the target.
6. **Send. Log send date in this tracker.**

## Response time targets

- First positive reply → respond within 2 hours (LinkedIn algo rewards early engagement on the thread)
- Call booked → come prepared with live PageSpeed screenshot + 3 quick-win recs + 1 "what we'd do differently" recommendation
- No reply after 10 days → ONE follow-up with a blog link (draft in `outreach-drafts-week-2.md`), then drop
